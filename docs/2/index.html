The second international workshop on Pomsets and Related Structures took place in Rzeszów 30 June -- 4 July 2025.

The workshop is associated with the (i)Po(m)set Project, a research project at the crossroads of concurrency theory, automata theory, algebra, and geometry. This second edition follows a RaPS workshop held in Rennes in April 2024.

Program

  • 30 June: Directed model categories
  • 1 July: Action replacement
  • 2 July: Petri nets, HDAs, and other models
  • 3 July: Action replacement
  • 4 July: Directed model categories

Participants

  • Uli Fahrenberg, LRE, EPITA Rennes, France
  • Christian Johansen, NTNU, Gjøvik, Norway
  • Georg Struth, The University of Sheffield, UK
  • Krzysztof Ziemiański, University of Warsaw, Poland

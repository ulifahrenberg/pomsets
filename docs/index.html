Workshop on
Pomsets and Related Structures

The Workshop on Pomsets and Related Structures will take place at EPITA Rennes on 24 April 2024.

The workshop is associated with the (i)Po(m)set Project, a research project at the crossroads of concurrency theory, automata theory, algebra, and geometry. The project also has an online seminar, and the workshop is an offshoot of that seminar.

Venue

The workshop will take place at the Viasilva campus of EPITA Rennes, 3679 Boulevard des Alliés, Cesson-Sévigné.

Call for contributions

We are calling for presentations of original, unfinished, already published, or otherwise interesting work which can highlight how the research topics of the workshop may be developed. Note that the workshop will have no formal proceedings.

Please send your proposal, in the form of a pdf file of 1-3 pages, to fahrenberg@gmail.com by 1 March 2024.

Important dates

  • Abstract submission: 1 March 2024
  • Author notification: 10 March 2024
  • Workshop dates: 24 April 2024

RaPS

The third international workshop on Pomsets and Related Structures will take place at the Plateau de Saclay, south of Paris, on 11 and 12 September 2025. It is collocated with an edition of the Paris ACTS.

The workshop is associated with the (i)Po(m)set Project, a research project at the crossroads of concurrency theory, automata theory, algebra, and geometry. This third edition follows RaPS workshops held in Rennes in April 2024 and in Rzeszów in June 2025.

Venues

The workshop will take place at ENS Paris-Saclay (Thursday 11 September) and at École polytechnique (Friday 12 September).

Registration

Registration for RaPS #3 is free but mandatory. Please register before 1 September using the form at https://framadate.org/eE6Bm4EqOkrtEw9q.
